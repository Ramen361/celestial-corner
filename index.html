About Me

Hi, I’m Ramen (She/Her), a 14-year-old freshman with a passion for digital creation, web development, design, and interactive media. I enjoy combining creativity and functionality to build projects that are visually appealing, interactive, and user-friendly. I thrive on learning new skills, exploring innovative ideas, and collaborating with others to bring concepts to life.

Alongside my technical pursuits, I have a strong interest in psychology and mental health, which informs my personal growth and the empathetic perspective I bring to my work. I enjoy journaling, therapy, climbing, walking, running, biking, and reading, activities that help me stay balanced, inspired, and reflective. I am also known for being trustworthy and non-judgmental, and many people feel comfortable confiding in me and relying on my support, which I take seriously and value deeply.

Music and media play a major role in my life. My tastes range from Spinvis, ppcocaine, Rebzyyx, Eminem, The Weeknd, Radiohead, James Arthur, Shinedown, Ashnikko, D4vd, XXXTENTACION, Chase Atlantic, Black Atlass, Odetari, Mila, reflecting both my emotional depth and appreciation for experimental sounds. I also enjoy films and shows such as the Twilight series, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Pitch Perfect (1–3), and Remember Me, which influence my storytelling and creative vision.

I value quiet moments, rain, nighttime, and solitude, finding inspiration in reflection and imagination. I enjoy coffee, tea, boba, Monster drinks (orange cream/dreamsicle), bees, fairycore and dark fairycore aesthetics, and jewelry or accessories that allow me to express individuality. At the same time, I avoid loud or disrespectful environments, insects, makeup, celery, and Sprite, as these disrupt focus or comfort.

Overall, I aim to grow continuously, explore my interests fully, build trusting connections with others, and create work that is meaningful, inspiring, and reflective of my personal vision.
